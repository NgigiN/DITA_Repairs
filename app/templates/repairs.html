DITA serves its members and the rest of the Daystar University community with repairs. DITA members get a special discount! The DITA repair process is as follows:

  1. You make a request online for a repair.
  2. You deliver your laptop to the DITA office.
  3. Keep track of your repair from your profile section until it reaches "done".
  4. You make your payment and input your transaction code in the profile section.
  5. Pick up your device as soon as possible.

DITA aims to be as transparent as possible as we service each repair.
